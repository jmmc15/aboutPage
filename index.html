The eBOTO team is composed of 2nd year Information Technology students from the University of Santo Tomas. Inspired by the recent U.S. elections together with the upcoming 2022 elections, the team decided to come up with a solution for distance voting due to the current situation that the country is facing today. Guided by this, the concept of eBOTO was born, the team aims to create an application that can be of help to any kind of voting acitvities in order to lessen the burdens and struggles of doing a traditional one.
